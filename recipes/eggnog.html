Amazingly Good Eggnog

Description

This eggnog recipe for thick, creamy, and sweet eggnog with just the right amount of spice took several years to perfect. The egg yolks are tempered for safety, and you can adjust the amount of rum to taste, or leave it out entirely so the whole family can enjoy this rich holiday treat. It's a bit of work to make but well worth it. Once you taste it-you'll never serve store bought eggnog again!

Ingredients

  • Milk and light cream.
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Egg yolks
  • Sugar
  • Rum(Optional)

Steps

  1. Heat the milk, cinnamon, cloves, and a dash of vanila. Boil, then remove from heat.
  2. Whisk the egg yolks, then whisk in the sugar.
  3. Gradually whisk the hot milk mixture into the egg mixture.
  4. Cook the mixture over medium heat until thick, then strain to remove the cloves.
  5. Stir in the cream, rum(if you choose), remaining vanilla, and nutmeg. Refrigerate overnight.
